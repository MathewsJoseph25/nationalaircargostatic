Our Services

At National we adopt a holistic and innovative approach in offering our customers a complete cargo transport solution. Our services include Air Freight forwarding, ocean Freight forwarding, ground transport and warehousing. Our multimodal capabilities ensure that we deliver rapid solutions by air, sea and ground. With constant evolutions and rapid progress, the freight forwarding, we believe to be a step ahead an offer a complete and affordable cargo transport solutions to all our customers and partners.

The National multimodal network is an expansive, interwoven global supply chain that includes air transport, ocean freight, overland transport, even warehousing and storage.

Our Fleet

National has 5 Boeing 747-400 Freighters with registrations N 756CA, N 702CA, N952CA, N919CA, N729CA and acquisition plan in place for expansion, alongside the Airbus 330-200 and Boeing 757-200 in its fleet. Our expanded fleet includes a matrixed blend of long-term leased aircraft including: AN-26, AN-12, L-100, IL-76, A-300, B747, AN-124 and AN-225

Our Multimodal facility

Air Freight Forwarding

For around 3 decades, National is known in the industry for its impeccable track record in Air freight forwarding to anywhere in the world. Our organic fleet of advanced 747-400 F’s and an expanded fleet of long term leased aircrafts including AN-26, AN-12, L-100, IL-76, A-300, B747, AN-124 and AN-225, ensure that we have seamless transport solution for all customers. Our experienced pilots, crew and ground staff are trained to operate in a time bound manner in were remote and challenging destinations across the globe. Strict Adherence to FAA standards defines a professional and standardized proven practice in all our missions.

Ocean Freight forwarding

National is a licensed Non vessel Operating Common Carrier (NVOCC) provider. We provide ocean services that complement our air expertise providing a one-stop solution to meet all multimodal transportation needs. We offer Full Container Load (FCL) shipments, Less-than-Container Load (LCL) shipments and Temperature-Controlled Container Load shipments through our strategic partners. We are experts in Roll On Roll Off, Break – Bulk, Oversized Cargo management, Charter vessels including oil, Natural Gas, Dry bulk and liquid bulk.

Warehousing

National Air Cargo is the first US company to open a full warehousing and cross dock operation with the Dubai World Central (DWC) Logistics City. We provide full-service warehouse management to augment and support our cargo delivery solutions. We offer inventory receipt, inspection, and verification services coupled with the provision of our proprietary warehouse management IT systems wherever our customers require our presence. We provide warehousing solutions to customers, with specialized focus on rapidly moving air freight from our warehouse location to the air terminals. In addition, we maintain a secure outdoor storage yard that is available for both long term and short term storage needs to complement our indoor warehousing facilities. With National Air Cargo, we meet your warehousing requirements with tailored global solutions that ensure that your precious cargo is secured, stored, and handled with the utmost care

Our warehousing services also include cross dock count and condition verification, full range of receiving, inspection and verification services, packing, re packing, in house carpentry, crating, HAZMAT, controlled cargo and other specialized services for each customer requirement.
