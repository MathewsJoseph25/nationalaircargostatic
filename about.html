National Air Cargo is a global transporter that offers bespoke solutions to cargo operation anywhere in the world. Embracing the highest standards of quality, safety and security, we deliver your cargo in a rapid, unswerving and time definite manner. As a global leader in Multimodal and Freight Forwarding operations, we offer specially tailored solutions to ensure that every aspect of your business succeeds. With 3 decades of operational excellence behind us, we continue to Deliver the World to you, Anywhere, Anytime! Each National Air Cargo shipment is a customized experience. We offer responsive and personalized services in order to tailor specific transport solutions to our customers

National Air Cargo was founded by Chris Alf & Lori and commenced operation in 1991. In the initial years itself, the company gained the distinctive recognition as a regional freight forwarder in the USA. Over the last 3 decades Since inception, National grew to become one of the most preferred carriers and is in the elite category of world-class service providers in the logistics and transportation world. Acquiring the first fleet of aircrafts in the mid-2000s, the National group became one amongst very few companies that transformed from being a freight forwarder to a fully-fledged airline at the same time. As a US flag carrier, headquartered in Orlando, FL, the company acquired the Part 121 FAA license and obtained the Commercial Airlift Review Board certification shortly after. Furthermore, having become a Civil Reserve Air Fleet carrier, National is one of the prime United States Department of Defense transporters and earned its name as a “bespoke forwarder” in the industry. As a global airline, National strategically has operational hubs located in Frankfurt, Amsterdam, Dubai, Djibouti, Afghanistan, Kuala Lumpur, Tokyo, Hong Kong, and Shanghai, and currently employs over 500 people worldwide.

We just do not deliver your goods, we do it with pride, passion and proven performance indicators. Integrity, Trust, Fairness and Respect are key to our services and customer interactions. National Air Cargo trains all employees on the laws, regulations and company policies to ensure they understand the expected compliance, commitment and ethics required in our operations. National is proud to be a member of the Defense Industry Initiative on Business Ethics & Conduct and we encourage our industry partners, customers and vendors to explore the benefits of DII by visiting www.dii.org
